Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App

In today's digital age, access to healthcare information is critical. Remote healthcare solutions are becoming increasingly important, so we took on the challenge of creating a symptom-based disease diagnosis web app. Using Flask for the backend and a Decision Tree Classifier model, we developed a platform that helps users identify potential diseases based on their reported symptoms.

The Problem

People often experience symptoms and seek immediate answers. However, differentiating between illnesses can be difficult, especially when symptoms overlap. Our solution allows users to input their symptoms and receive potential diagnoses instantly, offering them peace of mind and direction.

The Solution

Our web app enables users to input their symptoms, and the platform uses a pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier model to predict possible diseases. Here’s how it works:

  1. Users input their symptoms via an intuitive, user-friendly interface.
  2. Our machine learning model predicts the most likely diseases based on the input.
  3. Users receive a prediction instantly, along with additional details like disease description, medications, diet, and workout tips.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple, clean UI designed for ease of use.
  • Accurate Predictions: A reliable machine learning model trained on a vast dataset provides precise predictions.
  • Comprehensive Information: In addition to predictions, the app offers detailed advice on treatments, medications, and lifestyle changes.
  • Educational Blog Posts: Users can read health-related blog posts to expand their knowledge and stay informed.

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App combines the power of machine learning and healthcare to provide users with accurate and convenient access to health information. It’s a significant leap forward in making healthcare more accessible to all.
